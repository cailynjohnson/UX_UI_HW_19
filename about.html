hi, i'm cailyn!

I’m a UX/UI designer and developer with a background in the arts. With a BA in theatre and computer science from Northwestern University, I specialize in fusing artistic and analytical thinking to create compelling user experiences in the arts & entertainment industry. Creativity, collaboration, and communication developed through the performing arts coupled with strong coding skills allow me to bring a unique perspective to any project from ideation to execution. My approach to user-centered design is rooted in human observance and empathy - skills strengthened through years of acting training. As a life long learner, human observer, and problem solver, I’m on a mission to discover what it means to be human and apply my insights to immersive user experience solutions that ensure clients thrive within their competitive landscape and users engage in digital products with confidence. When not engrossed in Adobe XD or Visual Studio Code, you can find me performing on stage, teaching fitness classes, or seeking out the best frozen lakes in Minnesota to ice skate on.
